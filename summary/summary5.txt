The much-anticipated sequel to the worldwide hit family film finds Paddington happily settled with the Brown
family in Windsor Gardens, where he has become a popular member of the community, spreading joy and
marmalade wherever he goes. 
While searching for the perfect present for his beloved Aunt Lucy's hundredth birthday, Paddington spots a
unique pop-up book in Mr. Gruber's antique shop, and embarks upon a series of odd jobs to buy it. But
when the book is stolen, it's up to Paddington and the Browns to unmask the thief.
